# MyPhonicsBooks — Meta Ads Marketing Plan (Islamic-Compliant)

## Critical: Marketing Visual Guidelines

### ❌ NEVER SHOW IN MARKETING
- Realistic human faces (photos of children, parents, people)
- Detailed character illustrations with full eyes
- Any imagery that could be used for idolatry or inappropriate gaze
- Music in any video content

### ✅ USE INSTEAD
- Book illustrations (Islamic-compliant: minimal/slit eyes only)
- Book covers and open pages showing story scenes
- Flat lays of printed books on surfaces
- Typography-first designs
- Abstract shapes, level colours, patterns
- Open books showing pages/text only

---

## Campaign Objective
Drive parent traffic to free phonics assessment → capture email → deliver 3 free books + Skool community access → convert to paid books via community or email upsell.

**Core Fear:** My child is behind / I don't know their level / I'm buying the wrong books.
**Core Passion:** My child will read confidently / I'll be a good parent / They'll love learning.

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** UK Muslim parents + general market, ages 25-44, children 4-8
**Location:** UK only
**Pain:** Confused about phonics levels, wasting money on wrong books, child losing confidence
**Desire:** Clear answer on reading level + matched books that work

---

## Creative Strategy: One Message Per Slide

### Video Hook Formula (15s)
**Slide 1:** Problem (3s) — "Wrong books kill confidence"
**Slide 2:** Solution (5s) — "Exact level in 3 minutes"
**Slide 3:** Offer (4s) — "3 free books + community"
**Slide 4:** CTA (3s) — "Try it →"

### Carousel Formula (5 Slides Max)
1. **Problem** — "Still guessing their reading level?"
2. **Pain** — "Wrong books = frustration"
3. **Solution** — "Matched to exactly what they can decode"
4. **Proof** — "Based on UK curriculum"
5. **CTA** — "3 free books + community →"

---

## Meta-Safe + Islamic-Safe Copy

### ❌ NEVER USE
- "Your child" (use "they" or names)
- Faces in imagery
- Urgency/pressure tactics
- "Limited time" / "Act now"
- Fear-mongering about "falling behind"

### ✅ USE INSTEAD
- "Find their level"
- "Matched to what they can decode"
- "Free to try"
- "Based on Letters and Sounds"
- Book/product imagery only (no faces)
