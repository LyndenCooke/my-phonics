# MyPhonicsBooks — Meta Ads Marketing Plan

## Campaign Objective
Drive parent traffic to free phonics assessment → capture email → deliver 5 free books → convert to paid custom books/subscription.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: UK Parents of Early Readers
- **Age:** 25-44
- **Location:** United Kingdom (focus: England, Wales, Scotland)
- **Parents with children:** 4-8 years old
- **Behaviours:** Engaged shoppers, Online course purchasers, Parents of school-age children
- **Interests:** Phonics, Reading, Early years education, KS1, Year 1 phonics check, Letters and Sounds, Parenting blogs, Educational toys

### Secondary: Teachers & Educators
- Primary school teachers
- Teaching assistants
- Homeschooling parents

### Exclusions
- Parents with children under 3 (too early)
- Parents with children over 10 (too late for phonics focus)
- Non-UK (different curriculum, shipping complexity)

---

## Funnel Architecture

```
AWARENESS (Cold Traffic)
├── Video Ad Hook (15s) → "Is [Name] ready for Level 2?"
├── Carousel Ad → "5 signs they're ready to level up"
└── Static → "Free phonics books matched to their level"
    ↓
LANDING PAGE
├── Headline: "Find Their Reading Level in 3 Minutes"
├── No signup required to START
└── CTA: "Start Free Assessment"
    ↓
ASSESSMENT (Child-facing, 3-5 mins)
├── Gamified level finder
├── Friendly character guide
└── Celebration at completion
    ↓
RESULTS PAGE (Email capture gate)
├── Personalised: "Emma is at Level 2 — New Sounds!"
├── Specific sounds they know/need to practise
└── CTA: "Get 5 FREE books for Emma"
    ↓
EMAIL DELIVERY
├── Instant: 5 free PDFs
├── Nurture sequence (7 emails over 14 days)
└── Soft upsell to custom/paid books
```

## Campaign Structure

### Campaign 1: Awareness (Top of Funnel)
**Budget:** 40% of ad spend
**Goal:** Reach, Link Clicks, Video Views

**Ad Sets:**
1. Parents of 4-6 year olds (Year R / Year 1 focus)
2. Parents of 6-8 year olds (catch-up readers)
3. Teachers / Early years educators

### Campaign 2: Consideration (Middle of Funnel)
**Budget:** 35% of ad spend
**Goal:** Landing Page Views, Assessment Starts
**Audience:** Engaged users (video views 50%+, carousel interactions, landing page visits)

### Campaign 3: Conversion (Bottom of Funnel)
**Budget:** 25% of ad spend
**Goal:** Email Captures, Free Book Downloads
**Audience:** Assessment completers, email subscribers (lookalike), website custom audiences

## Creative Strategy

### Video Hierarchy (3:4 ratio for Instagram/Facebook feeds)

**Hook Variants to Test:**
1. Problem-agitation: "Still searching for books at the right level?"
2. Curiosity gap: "We made 5 free books matched to their exact phonics level"
3. Social proof: "Used by 10,000+ parents across the UK"
4. Direct benefit: "Help [Name] read with confidence"

### Carousel Formula
- **Slide 1:** Hook question (problem aware)
- **Slide 2:** The gap (decodable books are generic, diverse books aren't phonics-structured)
- **Slide 3:** Solution (MyPhonicsBooks = intersection)
- **Slide 4:** Social proof/Trust ("Aligned with UK phonics curriculum")
- **Slide 5:** Free offer + CTA

### Static Ad Templates
- Book mockups with level colours
- Parent testimonial quotes
- "Free" badge prominent
- Child name personalisation examples

---

## Meta-Safe Copy Guidelines

### ❌ DON'T USE (Policy Risk)
- "Your child"
- "Your kid"
- "Struggling child"
- "Behind in reading"
- "Catch up" (implies failure)
- Limited time / urgency pressure
- "Act now" / "Don't miss out"
- Health/educational claims without qualifiers

### ✅ USE INSTEAD
- "Help [Name] read with confidence"
- "Find their reading level"
- "Matched to exactly what they can decode"
- "Free books for [Name]"
- "The best time to start is now"
- "Free to try"
- "Aligned with the UK phonics curriculum"
- "Based on Letters and Sounds"

## Budget Framework

### Test Phase (Weeks 1-4): £500-1000
- 3-5 ad creatives per campaign
- 3-5 audiences per campaign
- Daily budget: £20-35
- Kill anything under 1% CTR or over £2 CPC

### Scale Phase (Weeks 5-12): £2000-5000/month
- Double down on winning creatives/audiences
- Test lookalike audiences from converters
- Introduce retargeting sequences

### Optimise Phase (Month 3+): £5000+/month
- 80% budget to proven converters
- 20% to new creative/audience tests
- Dynamic creative optimisation (DCO)

## Retargeting Sequences

**CTA:** "Try the free assessment"

### Sequence 2: Landing Page (No Start)
**Timeline:** 1-14 days
**Creative:** Static with social proof
**CTA:** "Still curious? Start free →"

**Timeline:** 1-7 days
**Creative:** Video "It only takes 3 minutes"
**CTA:** "Complete assessment →"
