The Mystery Box

A story about curiosity and surprise
Focus sounds
tion ture kn wr str
Page 1
🏠🌤️

One bright morning, Kai found a strange box on the front step.

It was wrapped in brown paper and tied with string.

There was no name on it. There was no written note.

"That's odd," said Kai. "I know I didn't order anything."

Page 2
👧📦❓

Kai picked up the box and gave it a shake. Something shifted inside.

She felt a strong knocking sound, then a soft scratching.

"This is a real mystery," she said.

Her imagination began to run wild.

Page 3
👧🧓🪑

Kai took the box to Grandma's kitchen.

"I need your knowledge," said Kai. "What do you think is in here?"

Grandma put on her glasses and inspected the wrapping.

"There are instructions on the side," Grandma said. "Look — it says 'Open with care.'"

Page 4
✂️📦✨

Kai cut the string with scissors and peeled back the paper.

Inside was a wooden box with a small knob on the lid.

There was a tiny structure carved into the wood — it looked like a castle.

Kai felt a mixture of excitement and caution.

Page 5
👧🔓🎶

She turned the knob and the lid popped open.

A soft tune began to play. It was a music box!

Inside sat a tiny knight on a miniature horse, spinning in a circle.

"How wonderful!" Kai gasped.

Page 6
📝💌

Under the knight, Kai found a folded note.

The writing was neat and small.

It read: "Dear Kai, this was mine as a child. It gave me great pleasure. I hope it brings you joy too. With love, Grandpa."

Kai looked at Grandma, who was smiling.

Page 7
🧓💕👧

"Grandpa wrote that before he went on his trip," Grandma explained.

"He wanted you to have this treasure while he's away."

Kai held the box close.

"I knew it was something special," she whispered.

Page 8
👧📦🌟⭐

That night, Kai placed the mystery box on her shelf.

She wound the knob and let the gentle tune fill the room.

The tiny knight turned in slow circles, catching the moonlight.

Kai smiled. Some mysteries have the best solutions.

🌟

The End!
